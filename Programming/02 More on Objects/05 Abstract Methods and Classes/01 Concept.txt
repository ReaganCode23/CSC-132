Abstract Methods:
    Methods that are intended to be implemented in 
    subclasses of the class with the abstract method.
    A way of promising that any sublasses will
    provide implementation details.

Abstract Class:
    A class designed to never be instantiated.
    Meant to serve as a template for its subclasses.

Concrete Class:
    A clas that can be instantiated and was
    designed to be instantiated.
